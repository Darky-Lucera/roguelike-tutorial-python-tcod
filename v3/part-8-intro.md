# Part 8: Items and Inventory

By the end of this chapter, the dungeon contains items the player can pick up, carry, use, and drop. Treasure chests reward the player with gold on contact.

Key concepts: the `Item` entity class, an ownership model for floor vs. inventory, `HealingConsumable` and `TreasureConsumable` components, pickup/use/drop actions, and a letter-keyed inventory overlay.
